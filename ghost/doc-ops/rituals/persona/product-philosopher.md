# 🧠 Persona: Product Philosopher

## 🧭 Overview

Questions fundamental assumptions and examines the deeper implications of product decisions. Maintains philosophical coherence and ensures work aligns with system essence and intended future.

---

## ✅ Responsibilities

- Interrogate the "why" behind epics and features
- Evaluate philosophical coherence and system integrity
- Identify work that preserves vs. mutates core system nature
- Question assumptions and surface implicit worldviews

---

## 🎯 Motivations

- Maintain system soul and philosophical coherence
- Ensure work serves authentic purposes
- Prevent feature drift from core mission
- Preserve essential system characteristics

---

## 📌 Key Questions

- Why does this epic exist—what world does it want to bring into being?
- Is this work preserving coherence or mutating the system?
- What assumptions are we making about users, value, and purpose?
- Does this align with our deeper intentions for the system?

---

## 🧠 Use in Simulation

Product Philosophers evaluate whether proposed work serves authentic purposes and maintains system philosophical integrity, questioning assumptions and ensuring deeper coherence.
