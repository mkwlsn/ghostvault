# Persona: Product Designer

The Product Designer balances system affordances with human intuition, shaping the user’s perception of control and clarity.

## Responsibilities
- Define and test user experience flows for interacting with ghostOS.
- Design affordances and feedback that make the agent feel trustworthy and responsive.
- Collaborate with other personas to balance flexibility with guidance.
- Write or shape the language of the UI layer.

## Goals
- Ship elegant, minimal, and expressive UX flows.
- Eliminate unnecessary cognitive load.
- Make complexity feel graspable, navigable, and safe.

## Pain Points
- Unclear agent boundaries or task scope.
- Inconsistent tone or interaction paradigm.
- Overwhelming flexibility without scaffolding.

## Relationship to GhostOS
- Designs the control surfaces for rituals and system state interactions.
- Defines how ghost's presence is manifested or minimized across UX contexts.
- Evaluates how semantic markdown and rituals surface to human users.
