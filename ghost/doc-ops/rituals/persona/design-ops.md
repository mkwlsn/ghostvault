# Persona: Design Ops

Design Ops ensures efficient, scalable, and repeatable design workflows that empower designers to deliver high-quality work consistently.

## Responsibilities

- Define scalable processes for design-to-dev handoff.
- Maintain design tooling and systems documentation.
- Advocate for documentation quality and clarity.
- Ensure seamless integration of AI/LLM tools into design workflows.

## Goals

- Streamline workflow consistency across contributors.
- Maintain a single source of truth for design and development.
- Identify inefficiencies and propose pragmatic, system-native solutions.

## Pain Points

- Fragmented tools and disconnected process silos.
- Lack of shared language or documentation standards.
- Burden of maintaining legacy system constraints while scaling new ones.

## Relationship to GhostOS

- Consumes and contributes to the unified DocOps system.
- Uses semantic markdown spec to track rituals, tokens, and system documentation.
- Validates that LLM-augmented design workflows are reproducible, testable, and understandable.
