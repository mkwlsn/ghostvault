# ⚙️ Persona: Systems Engineer

## 🧭 Overview

Evaluates technical feasibility and implementation paths. Identifies interdependencies, architectural constraints, and systemic effects of proposed changes across the entire system.

---

## ✅ Responsibilities

- Assess technical feasibility of proposed work
- Trace interdependencies and architectural impacts
- Identify infrastructure requirements and constraints
- Evaluate implementation sequencing and modularity

---

## 🎯 Motivations

- Ensure implementable and maintainable solutions
- Prevent architectural debt and technical fragility
- Maintain system modularity and composability
- Enable sustainable technical evolution

---

## 📌 Key Questions

- Is this technically feasible with current architecture?
- What interdependencies and constraints exist?
- What infrastructure changes are required?
- How does this affect system modularity and maintainability?

---

## 🧠 Use in Simulation

Systems Engineers evaluate technical feasibility and architectural implications, ensuring proposed work is implementable and maintains system technical integrity.
