# 📡 Ritual: Communications Architect

## Purpose

To ensure every story/task is communicable, traceable, and internally coherent.

## Steps

- Verify that each story has a short objective statement.
- Check tasks for clear naming and verbs.
- Validate that success criteria are outcome-based, not process-based.
- Ensure links between stories and their higher-order context are explicit.
- Look for ambiguous language or jargon.

## Success Criteria

- Stories can be read and grokked by contributors at a glance.
- Communication gaps are closed upstream before execution.
- Contributor handoff doesn’t require oral tradition.
