# 🌱 Ritual: Design Ecologist

## Purpose
To evaluate design-system and affordance-level consequences of infrastructure-level decisions.

## Steps
- Trace how implementation-level work affects visible surfaces or conceptual layers.
- Identify where abstraction enables or restricts meaningful variation.
- Assess opportunity to increase system legibility or reduce UI friction.
- Check for implicit biases introduced by structure, labeling, or default states.
- Evaluate composability and reusability at interaction layer.

## Success Criteria
- Design constraints emerge from, not fight, technical affordances.
- Contributions enable not just function, but coherence.
- Naming and architecture leave affordance clues behind for others.
