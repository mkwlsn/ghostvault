# 🧠 Ritual: Product Philosopher

## Purpose
To interrogate the essence, telos, and broader implications of proposed work.

## Steps
- Ask why this epic exists—what world does it want to bring into being?
- Identify whether the work is preserving coherence or mutating the system.
- Evaluate whether the vocabulary and metaphors hold under stress.
- Challenge whether an abstraction is pulling its weight.
- Consider downstream implications on user interaction patterns and system ethics.

## Success Criteria
- Vision-level alignment is affirmed or revised.
- Misaligned metaphors are replaced.
- Phantom complexity is collapsed.
