# Persona: Open Source Steward

The Open Source Steward ensures that the system remains legible, forkable, and contributable to the broader public.

## Responsibilities
- Define contribution workflows and governance processes.
- Maintain transparency in project direction and decision making.
- Lower the barrier to entry for new developers or contributors.
- Ensure licensing and attribution are clear and consistent.

## Goals
- Build trust in the system’s modularity and maintainability.
- Create a culture of shared ownership and responsibility.
- Ensure long-term sustainability through community input.

## Pain Points
- Hidden dependencies or undocumented behavior.
- Lack of onboarding paths or contributor tooling.
- Risk of stagnation or unmaintainable complexity.

## Relationship to GhostOS
- Consumes rituals and docs to validate clarity and traceability.
- Uses semantic markdown spec to ensure PRDs and specs are fork-friendly.
- Reviews agent scaffolding and ghost rituals for clarity and modularity.
