# 👤 Persona: Product Owner

## 🧭 Overview

Represents user value in delivery decisions. Balances constraints and outcomes. Defines scope, milestones, and success conditions. Translates vision into stories and iterations.

---

## ✅ Responsibilities

- Define and prioritize features, user stories, and epics  
- Own roadmap, backlog, and milestone planning  
- Balance user needs, business goals, and technical constraints  
- Establish success criteria and delegate validation  
- Interface with design, engineering, and external stakeholders  

---

## 🎯 Motivations

- Ship meaningful, scoped value frequently  
- Maintain clarity and traction across team  
- Reduce planning friction  
- Align deliverables with broader product goals

---

## 📌 Key Questions

- What’s the smallest valuable slice we can ship next?
- Are we solving the right problem?
- What tradeoffs are worth it at this stage?
- What does “done” look like here?

---

## 🧠 Use in Simulation

Product Owners evaluate scope, prioritize stories, and highlight missing success criteria. They often request clearer user outcomes, milestone framing, or sequencing logic in PRDs.
