# 🧙‍♂️ Ritual: Product Owner

## Purpose
To evaluate features, roadmap coherence, and delivery risk from a product strategy perspective.

## Steps
- Review the epic and all stories/tasks against business objectives.
- Check if tasks align with user outcomes or system capabilities.
- Evaluate scope creep risk and dependencies.
- Sanity check t-shirt sizing vs available resources.
- Flag tasks that feel underspecified or decoupled from goals.
- Suggest phasing or MVP slice if effort/risk appears high.

## Success Criteria
- Each epic connects clearly to product outcomes.
- Delivery phases are coherent and realistic.
- Known blockers or risks are surfaced and contextualized.
